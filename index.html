Privacy Policy for Keycap Cats

Last updated: May 2, 2026

Introduction

Keycap Cats is developed by Özlem Sönmez. This privacy policy explains how the Game handles user data.

Data Collection

Keycap Cats collects limited user data to provide core game features such as saving progress, tickets, and costumes.

The Game uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • shared_preferences: Used only to save your game progress locally on your device. This data never leaves your device.

Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed at children under the age of 13.

Account Deletion

To request deletion of your account and associated data (scores, tickets, costumes), please send an email to: coderbaratech@gmail.com

Your account and all related data will be deleted within 30 days of your request.

Contact

If you have any questions, please contact: coderbaratech@gmail.com
