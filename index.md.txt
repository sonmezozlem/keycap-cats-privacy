# Privacy Policy for Keycap Cats

Last updated: May 2, 2026

## Introduction

Keycap Cats ("the Game") is developed by Özlem Sönmez. This privacy policy explains how the Game handles user data.

## Data Collection

Keycap Cats does not collect or store any personally identifiable information.

The Game uses the following third-party services:

- **Google AdMob**: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve ads. Please refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **shared_preferences**: Used only to save your game progress locally on your device. This data never leaves your device.

## Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed at children under the age of 13.
